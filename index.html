Data Analyst: A Data Analyst collects, processes, and analyzes data to uncover insights that help businesses make informed decisions. They use tools like SQL, Python, Excel, and data visualization platforms (e.g., Tableau, Power BI) to clean data, identify trends, and create reports or dashboards. Their role involves interpreting complex data, optimizing processes, and providing actionable recommendations to improve efficiency, performance, and business outcomes.
